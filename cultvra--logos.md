## Ontological Sketch

The system is not just a technical organism (Nervous, Muscular, Skeletal); it requires a **Society that Responds**.

### The Society of Action
- **The Role:** To critique, make art, attempts to know, and respond to the outputs of the technical organs.
- **The Gap:** Nothing utilitarianly exists with meaning and purpose unless it is used. Screenplays in drawers vs. active performance.
- **The Recording:** Pre-historic us is the un-recorded us. History begins with the record, but we must avoid the trap where the record becomes more important than the living action.

- Summon 3 critical lenses (Insider, Outsider, Adversary) for every major architectural move.
- Review the "Membrane" (Snowman metaphor) — the cold that kills those outside the system vs. the internal "misery" that is just the system doing its job.
